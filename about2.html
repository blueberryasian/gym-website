About Us

Founded by Colin and Jelly Muth

Where the Gym feels like home

Welcome to CJ Fitness, where passion, commitment, and love for fitness converge! We are a premier gym founded by a dynamic couple, Colin and Jelly, who share a common vision of empowering individuals to lead healthier and more fulfilling lives through fitness!


At CJ Fitness, we believe that fitness is not just a hobby; it's a way of life. Our mission is to instill this mindset in each of our members, fostering a community that thrives on encouragement, positivity, and progress. We are dedicated to offering a diverse range of fitness programs and classes, designed to challenge and engage individuals of all fitness levels.
